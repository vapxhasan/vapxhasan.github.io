01 Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Hasanur Rahman (operating online as @vapxhasan, referred to as "I", "me", or "this site") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this portfolio website or reach out about video editing, AI creative, or digital marketing services.

This site is a personal portfolio and freelance service showcase — it is not an e-commerce store, and no payment or checkout happens directly on it. Any information collected is used solely to communicate with you about potential projects and to operate the site itself.

02 Information We Collect

I only collect information that you choose to share, or that is technically necessary to make the site function correctly.

  • Contact form details — when you submit the contact form, your name, email address, and message content are sent to me via a third-party email delivery service so I can reply.
  • Direct outreach — if you message me on WhatsApp, email, or book a call through Calendly, I receive whatever information you choose to share on those platforms, governed by their own privacy terms.
  • Testimonials & reviews — if you submit a client review, your name, review text, and any rating you provide may be displayed publicly on the site.
  • Local preference data — your device may store a small amount of non-personal data locally (like your dark/light theme choice) to improve your browsing experience. This never leaves your browser.

I do not knowingly collect payment card details, government IDs, or other sensitive personal data through this website.

03 How I Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and discuss potential projects
  • To schedule calls or consultations you request
  • To display testimonials you have consented to share publicly
  • To keep the site secure, functional, and free of abuse
  • To remember basic display preferences such as theme mode

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

05 Cookies & Local Storage

This site uses minimal local storage rather than tracking cookies for advertising purposes. For full details, see the dedicated Cookie Policy.

06 Data Retention

Contact form messages and email correspondence are kept only as long as needed to respond to you and maintain reasonable business records, after which they may be deleted. Publicly displayed testimonials remain visible until you request their removal.

07 Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data I hold about you, or to withdraw consent for a testimonial to be displayed publicly. To exercise any of these rights, just get in touch using the details below — I aim to respond within a reasonable timeframe.

08 Children's Privacy

This site and the services offered are intended for businesses and individuals capable of entering into a professional agreement. It is not directed at children, and I do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16.

09 Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in how the site operates or to stay current with good practice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.
