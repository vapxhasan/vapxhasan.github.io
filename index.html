FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before we start working together.

How long does it take to complete a project? ▾
Most projects are delivered within 2–7 business days depending on scope. Rush delivery is available. I'll give you a clear timeline after reviewing your brief — before any work starts.
Do you offer revisions? ▾
Yes. Standard packages include 2–3 revision rounds. I aim to nail the brief on first delivery, but revisions are part of a healthy creative process — your satisfaction is the goal.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
I accept payments through Fiverr, Upwork, PayPal, and direct bank transfer. All platforms offer buyer protection. Pricing is project-based and agreed upfront — no hidden fees.
Can I see examples of your previous work? ▾
Absolutely! The portfolio section above shows selected projects. I can also share additional niche-specific samples during our initial conversation. Just ask!
Do you work with international clients? ▾
Yes, 100%. I work with clients from the US, UK, Europe, Middle East, Canada, and Australia. I'm fluent in English and communicate clearly across time zones. Remote-first is my default.
What AI tools do you use? ▾
I use the best tools per category: Midjourney, DALL·E for images; Sora, Runway, Kling for video; ElevenLabs, Suno for audio; ChatGPT, Claude for automation; Make.com, Zapier, n8n for workflows.
Will I own the rights to the final work? ▾
Yes. Upon full payment, all rights to the final delivered files transfer to you. I retain the right to display work in my portfolio unless you require confidentiality, which I fully respect.
