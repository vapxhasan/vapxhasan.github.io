01 What Are Cookies & Similar Technologies

Cookies are small text files that websites can place on your device to remember information. Related technologies like localStorage and sessionStorage work similarly — they let a site remember small bits of data inside your own browser, without that data being sent anywhere else.

02 What This Site Actually Uses

This site is intentionally lightweight when it comes to tracking. Here is exactly what is stored on your device and why:

Neither of these is used for advertising, cross-site tracking, or building a profile of you. They are purely functional and stay on your device.

03 Third-Party Cookies From Embedded Content

Some parts of the page embed content from other platforms, and those platforms may set their own cookies once you interact with them:

  • YouTube — embedded video players may set cookies once you press play, governed by Google's cookie policy.
  • WhatsApp & Calendly — if you click through to chat or book a call, those platforms may set their own cookies on their domains.
  • Google Fonts — loading web fonts involves a request to Google's font servers, which may log basic technical request data.

These third-party cookies are controlled by their respective providers, not by this site directly.

04 Managing Your Preferences

Because this site avoids advertising and cross-site tracking cookies, there is little to opt out of — but you always have full control:

  • Use the theme toggle at the top of any page to change your stored preference at any time.
  • Clear your browser's site data or localStorage to reset all locally stored preferences instantly.
  • Adjust your browser settings to block third-party cookies from embedded platforms like YouTube.

05 Changes to This Policy

If the technologies used on this site change — for example, if analytics or advertising tools are added in the future — this policy will be updated accordingly, along with the "Last updated" date above.
